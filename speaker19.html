Investment Management Panel



Scott Radell ‘92 +

Scott Radell ‘92

BlackRock

Scott Radell, Managing Director, is the Head of San Francisco Core Portfolio Management (Core PM) within Systematic Fixed Income, responsible for the management and oversight of all the Americas Index, iShares, and model based active fixed income funds. Mr. Radell's service with the firm dates back to 2003, including his years with Barclays Global Investors (BGI), which merged with BlackRock in 2009. Before founding the Portfolio Solutions Group, he was a portfolio manager responsible for BGI's active investment grade long-only and long/short cross-over portfolios. Prior to joining BGI, Mr. Radell served for over seven years as an analyst for corporate bond and Commercial Mortgage Backed Securities for Morgan Stanley Investment Management. Mr. Radell began his career as a fixed income client service and mortgage analyst at BARRA.

Alan Solursh ‘89 +

Alan Solursh ‘89

Morgan Stanley

Alan Solursh is a Managing Director and Financial Advisor with offices in both Beverly Hills and Woodland Hills. He and his team provide advice and counsel to a range of high net worth families, corporate executives, entertainment industry professionals, retirees, small business owners, foundations and endowments. Alan also is a member of Morgan Stanley Global Sports & Entertainment, a group dedicated to serving the complex needs of athletes, entertainers and top executives in the fields of sports and entertainment. Leveraging his extensive background and experience in behavioral finance, Alan takes the time to educate his clients on the emotional challenges of wealth management, and designs tailored strategies to help clients meet their goals.

Jeff Witt +

Jeff Witt

Private Asset Management

Jeff Witt CFA CIPM serves as the Chief Investment Officer at Private Asset Management, Inc. In this role, Mr. Witt oversees the company’s investments process, global economic and financial analysis, asset allocation, and risk management of the firm’s portfolio. Prior experience has included working as a Senior Financial Analyst/Economist in the municipal bond arena for the City of San Diego and as an Equity Analyst for the investment bank Hanifen Imhoff. Mr. Witt graduated cum laude from the University of Colorado at Boulder with a B.A. in Economics and a B.A. in Philosophy of Law. He holds the Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA), the Certificate of Investment Performance Measurement (CIPM), and Certified Financial Planner (CFP) designations. Mr. Witt is regularly quoted in national and international business publications, including the Wall Street Journal, MarketWatch and other major news outlets.

Emmy Sobieski, ‘88 +

Emmy Sobieski, ‘88

Nicholas Investment Partners, DIGITA

Emmy Sobieski‘s two decades of research experience in finance, investments and innovative technologies provides investors and companies insights on competing in a rapidly changing world. As the CIO; Head of Customer Innovation of DIGITA, a software company for private markets asset managers, she is versed in strategizing on business use cases at the intersection of machine learning, distributed ledger technology (blockchain), and next-generation Fintech trends powering the digitization of private markets. Emmy’s buy side experience includes working for investment firms representing diverse growth strategies and asset size. She also brings decades of experience in commercial real estate transactions, land management and alternative energy projects. Emmy has focused on emerging and developing technologies for 24 years over a broad spectrum of fund sizes. Emmy is also the advising senior technology analyst at Nicholas Investment Partners, a San Diego RIA with $1.5B+ in AuM. She served as the VP and Sr. Director of Oppenheimer Funds, a leading global asset management firm with $249B AUM, where she provided technology research for the $32B in mid and large cap growth managers’ funds and convertibles bond funds.

Jono Anderson ‘95 +

Jono Anderson ‘95

KPMG

Jono Anderson is a Partner at KPMG based in San Diego in their Global Strategy Group specializing in growth and innovation strategy for companies in the aerospace & defense, automotive, transportation and other industrial manufacturing sectors. Jono has over 20 years of experience providing strategic growth and innovation solutions to companies with highly engineered products and services.
